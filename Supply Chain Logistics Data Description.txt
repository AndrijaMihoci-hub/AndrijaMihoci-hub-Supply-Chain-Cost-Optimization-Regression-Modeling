
Supply Chain Logistics

Assigning a route to each order

Source: Brunel University London by Tatiana Kalganova and Ivars Dzalbs

Data: 9215 orders, 14 variables

Available carriers: 1540 entries (9 groups), 11 variables

22 allowed links between the plants (warehouses) and shipping ports

2036 supported plant (warehouse)-product combinations

14 special cases, where plants (warehouse) is only allowed to support specific customer

19 plants (warehouses) capacities measured in number of orders per day

19 plants (warehouses) cost associated in storing the products in given warehouse measured in dollars per unit
